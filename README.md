# ☕ Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

Businesses generate thousands of sales transactions every month, but raw data alone does not help decision-makers.

This project transforms coffee shop sales data into actionable business insights using Power BI, enabling stakeholders to understand sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and product trends.

The goal was to create an interactive dashboard that helps businesses move from data collection to data-driven decision making.

---

## 🚨 Business Problem

The coffee business faced several challenges:

- Difficulty identifying top-performing coffee products.
- No clear visibility into monthly sales trends.
- Lack of centralized KPIs for monitoring business performance.
- Challenges in tracking order volume and customer spending behavior.
- Limited ability to compare product performance over time.

Without a dashboard, decision-makers had to rely on raw transactional data, making analysis slow and inefficient.

---

## 💡 Solution

To solve these problems, an interactive Power BI dashboard was developed that provides:

- Real-time business KPIs.
- Product-wise sales analysis.
- Monthly sales trend analysis.
- Order volume tracking.
- Interactive filtering by product category and month.

The dashboard allows stakeholders to quickly identify opportunities and make informed business decisions.
